About us

The chatbot developed by Dr.  Calmedica communicates by mail with the patient. She asks questions selected thanks to a learning system headed by the specialist and then automatically evaluates the answers provided. She can also suggest that the patient make an appointment as soon as possible in the event of an identified risk.

Remember that using this system is by no means equivalent to a consultation with a specialist. If the result you obtained is troubling you, make sure to visit your physician!

Dr. Calmedica offers, a multi-channel remote monitoring platform, which uses artificial intelligence to dialogue with patients and improve their follow-up. The founders are the perfect alliance between technological and medical know-how.

Dr. Calmedica relies on a passionate team of technical, commercial and health experts, with the aim of revolutionizing the healthcare system through artificial intelligence.
